About

about.txt

I'm a Research Scientist working with Julius Adebayo at Guide Labs, a product-focused AI research company in San Francisco dedicated to building interpretable and reliable AI technology.

I earned my PhD in Machine Learning from Auburn University in 2025, working with Anh Nguyen. I was a Presidential Research Fellow. Previously, I completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science at KAIST, South Korea.

My research focuses on making AI models interpretable to humans — enabling people to debug, understand, and ultimately trust AI systems. I've published at top venues including NeurIPS, CVPR, and TMLR.
